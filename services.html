MORE SERVICES BY BOLBALA TRUST-



★Food For Hungry People

Vigorous efforts are carrying on to get mute blessings of people of about 1200 to 1400, satisfied with sufficient food through our 3 active projects namely “Ann Sev Rath”, “Local Noon And Evening Food Service Section” and “Shivmani Food Section And Mahavir Sadavrat Centre” forn the benefits of blind, insane, physically handicapped, disabled and children of out of state labourers.

★Free Of Charge Library

Bolbala Trust, organised library is available in day time with sufficient daily news papers, magazines and books of various subjects in bulk quantity for the use of children, young age and old age. Many people are availing the benefits of this library to increase the knowledge and satisfy reading hobbies. Recently with the aim of reading habits amongst children free library is established.

★Roti Bank Yojna

This project is the very first project in Gujarat of collecting Rotis from place to plce from over 1000 to 3000 families and then distributing it everyday amongst the needy and slum people in specific areas of Rajkot City. The aim of the trust doing this project is spreading awareness in common people to be helpful in social service activities which would help us create a better version of Rajkot.

★Chhas Vitran Kendra

Bolbala trust is also well known as Paniwal Trust as it is providing free of charge service at Lok Melas, Samuh Lagna Ayojans, religious occasions, medical camps and many other public gatherings and events. Also in hot summer days the trust is distributing chhas to needy families through various centres and is showing it's resposibility towards the needy.





★Jivdaya Seva Project

The project is well managed by Bolbala Mahila Dhun Manadal to cater rotis, mild and khichdi to street dogs, food for birds, aata goli for fishes, namkins to birds and ladvas for cows at panjarapoles.

★Facilities Of Nihar Saman

The organisation is providing free of charge 24 hours required antimvidhi items on death of people every society. Electric dead body box is also provided to keep dead body in certain required cases.

★Traffic Control And Education

With the support of Rajkot Traffic Police arrangement and co-ordination are maintained to solve difficult traffic problems of the city, traffic control, traffic discipline and traffic awareness.

★Children Club

The aim of establishment of children club is to make children responsible towards to future of the nation, give them right direction and guidance, development in all the fields of life and take care to stop them from going in improper and wrong direction of value less life.



★Emergency Services

The sevabhavi volunteers are always ready to be helpful like machines at the time of national emergency, flood, heavy rainfall, cyclone, earthquake and any other natural calamities. The trust is undertaking many other projects over and above like Healthy Ukala Centre, Celebration of social and religious festivals etc.

★Summer Training Classes

The trust is undertaking various activities in the field of self dependence and self employment. In which summer training classes and professional training are very useful to the children and the ladies. Moreover by conducting sewing classes and computer classes at very relief rate an excellent service is being catered to establish self dependent society.

★Pathikashram

"Pathikashram" is established by The Rajkot District Collector and Laxmiben J. Shethia Trust, the management of which is accepted by Bolbala Trust. The responsibilities of this management is challenging, differential and incomparable. The relatives of patients of out of station are provided facilities of staying and dinning for both times at the negligible token charge of Rs.6=00 only. The superb service of the trust is in the form of blessings of many patients.

★Services Of Medical Instruments

Bolbala helps almost 60 to 70 needy patients daily by providing them use of free of charge latest medical instruments like oxygen cylinder, wheel chair, suction machine, water bed, air bed, toilet chair, walker etc. the supply is all over Saurashtra region including Rajkot city.



★Ambulance/Shab Vahini

Bolbala trust is providing ambulance and vehicle to carry dead body to-fro residence and hospitals 24 hours to-fro Rajkot to any part of India.This helps the needy and slum area peopl to make carry dead bodies easily and more carefully.

★Senior Citizens Seva Unit

The unit is established with the aim to give self satisfaction in doing some material activities and to fill the life with joy, happiness and enthusiasm at the time of 2nd inning of life of the people when they feel loneliness and aimless resulting in depression and confusion in every step of life.

★Rehabilitation Centre Physically Challenged

The centre is working for the physically challenged people of Rajkot City and rural area in the field of social, economic self employment, legal advice, rehabilitation, introduction fair for marriage wisher, instrument and tricycle distribution etc. This year more than 250 members are registered in this unit.
